What is the community incentive program?

DeFiChain-Promo follows 2 goals:
1. Offering high quality content for promoting defichain
2. Incentivize user for their active social media participation

Nobody likes to work for free and we think, that everybody who puts effort into promoting the defichain should get rewarded.
The community incentive progam aims to reach exactly this!
Rewarding twitter-activities with "promo points" which will be converted to DFI coins.

To do so, we developed a standalone algorithm which is capable to track and anaylze your defichain-related twitter activity.
Based on that result, you'll be rewarded with "promo points" which will, as stated above, be converted to DFI coins.

That means: We pay you DFI Coins for promoting defichain on twitter!
